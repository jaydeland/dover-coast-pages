Nutrition

From our Mothers, Fathers all the way to their puppies we ensure only the most premium of food is used. Giving the growing puppies all the important nutrients to ensure they start life on a firm foundation. This means later in life they will have far less issues with fragile bones or hip-issues. 

Our puppies are weighed and checked daily during their first 3-4 weeks of life to ensure each one gets the care it needs to grow and flourish. This checks include hip and joint movement to ensure there are no problems developing.
